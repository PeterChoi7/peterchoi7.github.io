Project 3

Face Morphing

Overview

In this assignment, I'll create a morph animation that transitions my face into someone else's face. A morph involves warping the shape of both images and blending their colors. I'll start by taking a picture of myself on a uniform background, making sure it's the same size and aspect ratio as my target face. Next, I'll morph my face (Picture A) into the target face (Picture B) by producing 45 frames, with frame 0 being my original face and frame 45 being the target face. I'll then create a video or animated GIF of the sequence, playing at 30 frames per second.
